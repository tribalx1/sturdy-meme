About the Conference

Bridging Science and Industry Excellence

SIL is a multidisciplinary scientific event that brings together researchers, academics, and industry professionals with shared interests in Industrial and Logistics Engineering.

The conference fosters high-level scientific exchange on modeling, optimization, enhancement, reengineering, and performance assessment of industrial and logistics systems across a wide range of application domains.

SIL'26 places special emphasis on intelligent and sustainable pharma-hospital supply chains, integrating AI, IoT, and digital twins to enhance forecasting, inventory management, and real-time monitoring.

Operational
Performance modeling and system optimization across industrial domains.
Informational
Data-driven decision support and digital intelligence frameworks.
Technological
Industry 4.0 and 5.0 technologies enabling smart manufacturing.
Decision-Making
Intelligent algorithms and multiagent systems for logistics control.
