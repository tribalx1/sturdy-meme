Author Guidelines

Submission Options

Option One
Full Paper

Reviewed for presentation at the conference and publication in the official proceedings. Eligible for selection into special journal issues.

Maximum length: 10 pages (including references and appendix)
Format: PDF only
Language: English
Published in: Springer Proceedings (DOI)
Option Two
Abstract Only

Reviewed for presentation at the conference. Included in the program schedule. Does not appear in the published proceedings.

Length: 200 to 300 words
Presentation: Conference only
Language: English
Registration: Same rate as full papers
ℹ

Important Notice

All submissions must be done through EasyChair. Authors must submit original work not under review elsewhere. Both submission types require at least one registered author by the early bird deadline. All accepted and presented full papers will be indexed in Springer with a dedicated DOI for the proceedings.
